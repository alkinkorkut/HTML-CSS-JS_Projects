Frequently Asked Questions About Video Games

Q: What is a video game?

A: A video game is an electronic game that involves a visual display and user interface to interact with a computer or gaming console.

Q: What are some popular video games?

A: Some popular video games include Minecraft, Fortnite, Call of Duty, FIFA, Grand Theft Auto, and Halo.

Q: What are the different types of video games?

A: There are several types of video games, including action, adventure, role-playing, simulation, sports, puzzle, and strategy games.
